CEC FOR HIGHSCHOOLERS

Learning has never been easy as we can imagine it. According to John Locke, at birth, the (human) mind is a 'blank slate' without rules for processing data, and...that data is added and rules for processing are formed solely by one's sensory experiences. At CEC, our priority is to “Nurture”, “Inculcate”, “Mould” and “Empower” students so they can unleash their potentials and blossom readily for the future. It is essential to note that we at CEC through our NIME curriculum is designed with these simple pedagogical approaches such as; constructivism, inquiry-based (heuristic style), reflective, collaborative, and integrative scheme where the “children” will harness their; Creative Thinking, Communication, Social-Emotional Development and STEM Skills. Through facets of workshops in storytelling, counselling, study abroad, coding, robotics, public speaking, 3D painting, gaming, creative writing, Rubik's cube, creative arts, touring etc., students will immerse themselves independently and will be self-reliant.

CEC FOR HIGHSCHOOL GRADUATES

As for high school graduates, we assist you in setting your footings and clearing your academic path(s) to the right college of choice on scholarships....Suffice to say, we are an education consultancy based in Lagos, Accra, Addis Ababa, Lusaka, Harare, Lome and Port Harcourt. We are professionally committed to preparing and nurturing high school students/graduates for college. Therefore, our fundamental objective is to mentor you through the college application process, providing you an active, tailored feedback, ensuring you present your charismatic personal image. Also, we are objectively invested in supplementing your high school curriculum that will enable you to handle the intellectual rigorousness of college. Thereby, we help you tackle local challenges in your community. Through leading workshops, providing students mentorship for projects, and exposing students to exciting new research and technologies, we aim to achieve success at the end of the day. Trust me, with our international experiences, college admissions are generally fun in communication with our international coaches. Certainly, this is always fascinating, informative, engaging and fruitful. By focusing on innovation, urbanism, social entrepreneurship, wellbeing and sustainability in workshops, we're compelling you to think of your role as a global citizen. And this will help you to take the scholarship exams to countries like USA, Russia, India, Italy, Germany, China, Ghana, Kazakhstan, Spain, Egypt and Lebanon etc., for free in the post COVID-19 world.

CEC FOR STUDENTS IN IDP CAMPS

Considering we live in a world of uncertainty and in this current world of adversities, we at CEC are at the helm of bringing quality education for free...to students in IDP camps as a way of giving back to our communities. We believe that quality education is not only for the privileged elites in our society but for all in the society irrespective of their social, financial and religious status in the society. And as such our educational model is built on non- discriminative vision. As a WAY of giving back to our community, we bring free and quality education to the less privileged in our community that will reach out with the best quality education to the less privileged children in all nooks and crannies of Nigeria, most especially those children in the IDP camps and the world at large for free. The model is dynamic in the sense that it can be implemented in any part of the world. Simply, one of the things CEC does for IDP students is to recruit volunteers (most especially passionate students from all disciplines) as student-teachers and match them to these underprivileged students in any localized area. I have devised this current educational project to restore the dreams of the students out there in my community whose dreams were shattered because of their circumstances.

Vision

We at CEC we engage in work that has the potential to contribute to the improvement of the educational Community by building children's cognitive, affective and psychomotor domain to accommodate the broader evolution and reconfiguration of their future.
